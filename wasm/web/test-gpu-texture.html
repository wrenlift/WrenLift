Smoke test — textured quad via @hatch:gpu (web)

Exercises the texture / sampler / bind-group additions to the web GPU plugin: builds a 2×2 RGBA texture (red, green, blue, yellow), creates a sampler, binds them through a bind-group, and renders a quad with a simple WGSL shader. Expect a four-quadrant Mondrian square in the dark stage below.
